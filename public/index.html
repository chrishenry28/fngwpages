Fret Not

Located in Brogue Pennsylvania we at Fret Not Guitar Works take pride in helping guitarists get the most out of their instruments. With skilled craftsmanship and a passion for tone, we offer professional repairs, setups, and modifications to keep your guitar sounding and playing perfectly. Your music matters—let us help your guitar sound its best. Not only do we work with guitars but we can look at and diagnose almost anything including amplifiers, effects pedals, speakers, and much more.

Fret Not Guitar Works

Mission: Backed by over 20 years of hands-on playing and repair experience, Fret Not Guitar Works is dedicated to helping musicians unlock their instrument’s fullest potential. We combine careful craftsmanship, meticulous setups, and custom modifications with expert diagnostics for guitars, amplifiers, pedals, speakers, and more—so every note you play sounds and feels its very best.
Vision: We envision a world where every guitarist—from bedroom strummers to stage performers—plays with confidence and joy, knowing their instruments and gear are perfectly tuned, tailored, and maintained to inspire creativity and elevate their music.
